PART 5 — A FEW PRO TIPS
-----------------------

1. PIN THE LAUNCH POST on Facebook (click the "..." on your post →
   Pin to Top of Page) so it's the first thing visitors see for the
   next 1–2 weeks.

2. TAG OR MENTION any past corporate clients in the launch post if
   appropriate — increases reach and engagement.

3. RESPOND TO COMMENTS within the first few hours of posting —
   Facebook and Instagram boost posts that get fast engagement.

4. CONSISTENT NAME — your handles (@thespotcafes / @spotcafes) will
   stay the same. The website URL is the only thing changing.

THE SPOT CATERING — GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE SETUP
==================================================

Hi Mandy! Your new website is live at denversbestcaterer.com 🎉

The single biggest free thing we can do to get you more local Denver
leads from Google is set up (or update) your Google Business Profile.
This is what makes you show up in Google Maps, in the "near me"
results, and in the side panel when someone Googles your business.

This guide walks you through it step by step. Plan for 20–30 minutes
the first time. Verification can take a few days, so do this soon.

---

PART 1 — DO YOU ALREADY HAVE A LISTING?
---------------------------------------

Before creating anything new, check if Google already has a profile
for The Spot Catering (it often auto-creates one from old data).

1. Go to: https://www.google.com/maps
2. Search: "The Spot Catering Denver"
3. Look at what comes up:

   • If you see a profile that looks like yours → go to PART 2A
     (Claim the existing listing)

   • If nothing relevant shows up → go to PART 2B
     (Create a new listing)

   • If you see an OLD or WRONG listing (wrong address, wrong phone,
     "permanently closed", etc.) → go to PART 2A and claim it so
     you can fix it. Don't create a duplicate.


PART 2A — CLAIM AN EXISTING LISTING
-----------------------------------

1. Click the listing in Google Maps to open it
2. Click "Claim this business" or "Own this business?"
3. Sign in with the Gmail account you want to manage it with
   → Recommended: spotcafes@gmail.com
4. Follow Google's prompts to verify ownership (see PART 4)
5. Once verified, go to PART 3 to fill in all the details


PART 2B — CREATE A NEW LISTING
------------------------------

1. Go to: https://business.google.com/create
2. Sign in with: spotcafes@gmail.com
3. Business name: The Spot Catering
4. When asked "What kind of business is this?":
   - Choose: "Service business" (you go to clients, they don't come to you)
   - This is important — it lets you serve a service area instead of
     publishing a physical address
5. Primary category: Caterer
6. Continue to PART 3 to fill in details

PART 4 — VERIFY YOUR BUSINESS
-----------------------------

Google has to confirm you actually own this business before the
listing goes public. They'll offer one or more of these methods:

  • Postcard by mail (most common, takes 5–14 days)
  • Phone call with a verification code
  • Text message
  • Email
  • Video verification (you record a short walkthrough)

Pick whatever's offered — phone/text/email are fastest.

If they ask for a postcard, the address you give them is private
(not shown on the listing) but it does need to be a real address
where you can receive mail.

⚠️ Don't edit anything else on the profile until verification is
complete — it can reset the process.


PART 5 — AFTER VERIFICATION (THE BIG WINS)
------------------------------------------

Once your profile is verified and live, do these to maximize visibility:

PHOTOS — Upload at least 10 great photos
  • Your logo (square version)
  • A cover photo (food spread or you in chef whites)
  • 5–10 photos of food, setups, themed events
  • A photo of you (Mandy) — Google promotes profiles with owner photos
  Pull these from your existing site (denversbestcaterer.com/gallery)
  or your phone. The more, the better.

REQUEST REVIEWS
  Click "Get more reviews" on your profile dashboard. Google gives
  you a short link like https://g.page/r/... — text or email this to:
    • Past corporate clients
    • Happy event hosts
    • Anyone who's complimented your food
  Aim for 5+ reviews in the first month. Even 10 good reviews
  dramatically boosts how often you show up in local searches.

POSTS (free advertising)
  Once a week, post an update on your profile:
    • A photo from a recent event
    • A seasonal menu special
    • A holiday booking reminder
    • A behind-the-scenes shot
  These show up in your Google listing and keep it active in Google's eyes.

PRODUCTS / SERVICES
  Add your menus as "Services" with prices or "starting at" pricing:
    • Corporate Lunch Catering
    • Themed Event Catering
    • Breakfast Catering
    • Happy Hour Catering
    • Crock Spot (your packaged offering)
  Each one can have a photo + description + link to denversbestcaterer.com


PART 6 — IF YOU GET STUCK
-------------------------

  • Profile help center: https://support.google.com/business
  • Common issue: "Suspended" listing → usually a category mismatch
    or address that conflicts with another listing. Edit and resubmit.
  • Verification not arriving? Request a new postcard from your
    profile dashboard.
  • Brett can help walk through any of this if needed.
